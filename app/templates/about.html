About CCTRS

The Civic Complaint Tracking & Resolution System is built to bridge the gap between citizens and municipal authorities.

"Together for a cleaner, safer community."

What Is CCTRS?

The Civic Complaint Tracking & Resolution System (CCTRS) is a web-based platform designed to:

Enable citizens to submit complaints easily from any device.
Allow departments to manage and track cases efficiently.
Provide transparency through a structured lifecycle.
Improve accountability in municipal civic service management.
