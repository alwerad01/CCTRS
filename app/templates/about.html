Who We Are

We are a team of Software Engineering students passionate about using technology to solve real-world civic problems. The Civic Complaint Tracking System (CCTRS) was developed as an academic project with a practical vision: to improve communication between citizens and municipal authorities through a structured, transparent, and efficient digital platform.

This system represents our combined efforts in Software Engineering, Database Systems, and Human-Computer Interaction, integrating technical implementation with user-centered design principles.

What Is CCTRS?

The Civic Complaint Tracking System (CCTRS) is a web-based platform designed to:

  • Enable citizens to submit complaints easily
  • Allow departments to manage and track cases efficiently
  • Provide transparency through a structured complaint lifecycle
  • Improve accountability in civic service management
